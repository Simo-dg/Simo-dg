## üöÄ Featured Projects

Here are a few projects I've built.

### 1. ‚úàÔ∏è Aircraft Delay Log Intelligence (NLP)
* Developed an end-to-end NLP pipeline to classify aircraft delay causes, operational phases, and predictability from free-text incident reports.
* Fine-tuned BERT models achieved a **1.00 F1-score** (delay type) and **0.71 F1-score** (phase).
* Integrated attention-based interpretability for token attribution and deployed the model as a Streamlit app.
* **[Tech:]** Python, PyTorch, Hugging Face (BERT), Streamlit

### 2. üì¶ Automated Pallet Counting (Computer Vision)
* Developed a multi-target regression system to count 'chep', 'epal', and 'total' pallets from warehouse images.
* Built and tested an ensemble of **EfficientNet-B3, ResNet-50, and ConvNeXt-Tiny**, achieving an **MAE of 1.15** and **R¬≤ of 0.95**.
* Used Grad-CAM for model explainability and extensive data augmentation for generalization.
* **[Tech:]** Python, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, Pandas, Grad-CAM

### 3. üìà Inflation Forecasting in Crisis Times

* Implemented Sequential Monte Carlo, MCMC sampling, and regime-switching dynamics.
* Neural networks achieved a **6.8% RMSE improvement** over benchmarks, while Bayesian methods provided superior uncertainty quantification.
* **[Tech:]** Python, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, Bayesian Modeling, R
